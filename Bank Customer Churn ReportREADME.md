## 6️⃣ Bank Customer Churn Report

### 📌 Business Problem (WHY)

Customer churn directly impacts bank revenue and growth. Banks need early identification of high-risk customers to design effective retention strategies.

### 📊 Dataset (WHAT DATA)

* Customer demographic data
* Account and transaction details
* Churn indicator

### 🧹 Data Preparation (HOW)

* Cleaned customer demographic attributes
* Encoded churn flags
* Segmented customers by behavior and profile

### 🎯 KPIs Defined (WHY THESE KPIs)

* Churn Rate → Overall risk level
* Active vs Churned Customers → Retention health
* High-Risk Segments → Targeted intervention

### 📈 Dashboard Design

* Churn overview dashboard
* Segment-level churn analysis

### 🔍 Key Insights

* Certain customer segments show significantly higher churn probability
* Engagement level strongly impacts churn behavior

### 🚀 Business Impact

Enables banks to design targeted retention campaigns and reduce revenue loss.
